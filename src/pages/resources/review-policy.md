## Review process overview

ML4H uses a double-blind review process: reviewers are unaware of author
identities, and authors are unaware of reviewer identities during the
review period.

### Review stages

- **Initial review.** Each submission receives reviews from multiple program committee members.
- **Author response.** Authors may submit a brief rebuttal addressing factual errors.
- **Discussion.** Reviewers and area chairs discuss submissions before making final recommendations.
- **Final decision.** Program chairs make accept/reject decisions informed by reviews and area chair recommendations.

### Reviewer assignments

Reviewers are assigned based on their stated expertise areas and declared
conflicts of interest. Conflicts with authors at the same institution or
recent collaborators are automatically excluded.

### Desk rejection

Submissions may be desk-rejected without review if they are significantly
out of scope, violate formatting requirements, or contain obvious ethical
violations.
