## Origins

Machine Learning for Health (ML4H) began as a workshop at NeurIPS,
bringing together a small but growing community of researchers applying
machine learning to problems in health and medicine. What started as an
informal gathering of pioneers in the field has grown into one of the
most respected venues for ML4H research.

### Growth and evolution

Over the years, ML4H has expanded its scope, community, and impact.
The workshop has consistently attracted high-quality submissions spanning
clinical prediction, medical imaging, natural language processing for
clinical text, and beyond.

Details on the full history of ML4H will be expanded here. In the meantime,
explore our [past events](/about/past-events/) for programs
and proceedings from previous editions.
