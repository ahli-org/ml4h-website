## About the mentorship program

ML4H is committed to supporting early-career researchers in machine
learning for health. Our mentorship programs connect junior researchers —
including PhD students and early postdocs — with experienced mentors from
academia and industry.

### Program details

The ML4H 2026 mentorship program details, including how to apply, eligibility
criteria, and the timeline, will be announced here. In previous editions,
the program has included:

- Paper mentorship: feedback on drafts before the submission deadline
- Career mentorship: structured conversations with senior researchers
- Affinity groups: community building among researchers with shared interests
