## About the demonstrations track

The ML4H demonstrations track provides an opportunity for researchers
and practitioners to present working systems, interactive tools, and
live prototypes that apply machine learning to health problems.

Demonstrations complement the paper track by highlighting practical
implementations, user interfaces, and real-world deployments that
show ML4H methods in action.
